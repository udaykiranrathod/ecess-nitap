Director's Message

Dear Students and Faculties,
I would like to congratulate the students for taking active part in establishing the Electronics & Communication Engineering Students Society (ECESS). Nonetheless, a joint effort from the faculties and staffs have culminated in establishing the departmental society. Since inception NIT Arunachal Pradesh has been striving for perfection in imparting knowledge through best practices in academia. ECESS will be an impetus not only in imparting quality education but also to established quality research outputs on cutting edge topics. I am proud to state that NIT Arunachal Pradesh has been placed in Band A (rank between 11-25) in ARIIA rankings 2020 among the category of Institute of National importance, central universities and CFTIs. In addition to that, Institute has secured 200th rank in NIRF 2020 rankings. Institute has signed 15 MoU’s at National and International level keeping in mind student’s future and to help them in accommodating the technological revolution. I believe that through ECESS you will have a platform to learn, explore, and engage to participate in an exciting journey of discovery and innovation.
I wish my best wishes to the Electronics & Communication Engineering Student Society.



HoD's Message

Dear Students,
Greetings from Electronics & Communication Engineering Student Society. Nelson Mandela once quoted “The greatest glory in living lies not in never falling, but in rising every time we fall.” With this esteemed vision for all the members of my ECESS family, I look forward to each one of you to strive ahead to fulfil your dreams with full compassion. I urge you all to move ahead as one unit and touch the peak you deserve in your respective career. Accordingly, we can grow and only grow if we are united and show hunger to achieve the vision. I pray and believe that our mutual growth will initiate a strong foundation of tremendous future possibilities. I henceforth want you all to be part of this momentous and significant organisation by contributing each day. You are the future and we trust your potential.
